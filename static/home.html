Also, and perhaps more importantly: The Skirts are a DIY community of weirdos who make sounds together and share in the feelings that emerge therein
The songs are, in short, emotional
And I'm not talking about the type of emotions you're trying to bottle up and hide; we mean the feelings that get shouted from the rooftops and spat in lovers' faces and spray painted onto old train trestles. The loudest, messiest, biggest, most uncomfortable feelings.
